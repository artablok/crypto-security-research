## Overview

Multiple scam tokens impersonating USDT have been detected on the Solana network.

These tokens appear in wallets via dust attacks.

## Risk

Users may believe they received legitimate USDT.

This can lead to phishing or malicious interactions.

## Recommendation

Wallet providers should:

• flag suspicious tokens  
• hide dust tokens  
• block impersonation tokens
